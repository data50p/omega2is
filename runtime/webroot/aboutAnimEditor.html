About Omega – interactive sentences – ver. 1.2

The Omega-is Animation Editor is a specially developed tool for creating and editing the animations that may be used to illustrate the meaning of the produced sentences.
The Omega-is animations are very flexible in that up to 12 different actors – with the additional option of referenced special sub versions of these actors – may be dynamically called in each animation during run-time, based on the words selected. In this way hundreds of different sentences may be illustrated by a small number of animations.
