About Omega – interactive sentences – ver. 1.2

Omega-is is a suite of three programs:
the main Omega-is Lesson part – for the language play activities
the Lesson Editor part – for editing and creating lesson material
and the Animation Editor part – for editing and creating Omega-is animations
